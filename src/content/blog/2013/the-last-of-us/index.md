**June 14, 2013** — The Last of Us is here and it's mine!

**June 15, 2013** — Wow, the 1st and 2nd part of the game felt so amazingly real, looking forward to playing some more today. Damn those Clickers are giving me a hard time and I don't want to spend too many resources on them.

**July 1, 2013** — Aaah damn it, 90% into The Last of Us and now I have to wait 3 more days until I can finish the last part — I seriously want to know how it ends!

**July 4, 2013** — Wow just wow, that was amazing. I am actually sad that I completed it, wishing for more. Also a nice little change in the main menu when you beat the game.

**July 9, 2013** — This fan art of Ellie from The Last of Us by Sara Muri is really nice and funny.

![The Last of Us fan art of Ellie](img-1.jpg)

**July 11, 2013** — Love The Last of Us multiplayer — I've been playing more for the fun than for the trophy I can get very soon.

**July 20, 2013** — Just completed my 2nd playthrough on Survivor+ and bam, all story trophies in the bag!
