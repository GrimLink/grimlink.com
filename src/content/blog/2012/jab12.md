**May 18, 2012 —** Currently waiting for the first session in the orange room — "Learn to love CSS3." Already seen this one at JD12NL but still nice to refresh my mind. Happy with the JAB12 Android app — lets you build your own list of sessions. The lunch was great and Coca-Cola in a glass bottle is always a nice touch. Amazing useful session by @Kyleledbetter.

**May 19, 2012 —** Good morning! Started with fog but clearing up. Been part of the "How to organize a Joomla event" session in the red room — more there to learn than discuss. The redShop session had a lot of information but really nice to hear. @brianteeman was dressed well for the JOSCAR awards.

**May 20, 2012 —** Last day of JAB12. Some interesting sessions today. The "Streamlining Joomla Template Deployment" session was interesting but a bit advanced for me. JAB12 is over — until next year for JAB13. Thanks Joomla community for this great weekend and all the advice.
