**April 21, 2012 —** Now at JD12NL, already crowded. The CCK ZOO presentation by @metdick was interesting. CSS3 session by Sander Potjer had some great examples. The migration 1.5 to 2.5 session was interesting but not that relevant to me. Only 1 slide at the "How to teach Joomla" session by @brianteeman — felt more like a podcast, watching my netbook screen and listening. First day over, many enjoying their beer.

**April 22, 2012 —** Good morning! Spending most of today in the Siteground room and one session in the Joomlatools room. The templating with Bootstrap & LESS session by @janssenstom was really interesting. The Super Mario SEO session by @theovdzee was funny. JD12NL has ended — already looking forward to JD13NL.
