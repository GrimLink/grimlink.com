I can not understand why people find the [Material Theme for VS Code](https://github.com/equinusocio/vsc-material-theme) so great.
The theme has bad contrast level.
And please don't tell me that there is a high contrast version — Material Design also states the requirements for contrast: https://material.io/design/color/text-legibility.html

If I had to recommend a Material Theme for VS Code, it would be [vscode-atom-material-theme](https://github.com/tobiasalthoff/vscode-atom-material-theme).
