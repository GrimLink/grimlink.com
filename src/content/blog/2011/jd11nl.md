**April 2, 2011 —** Now at JD11NL. Enjoyed the first session — up to the next one. Food was great. First day has been a great ride. WiFi died for a while but it's back now — big happy face. Spent the evening talking with other attendees about hardware, tech, and Joomla.

**April 3, 2011 —** Good morning! Great opening to kick off day two. Had to wait 25 minutes for the first session but the coffee here is really nice. JD11NL is great for learning but sometimes it's a lot to take in all at once. Really interesting session by @brianteeman about building Joomla. JD11NL has ended — great time, learned a lot about Joomla and everything that works with it. Already looking forward to next year.
